After, I went from building affordable houses and cycling across the Southern US one summer to ski instructing in Montana in the winter. I have since turned my attention to startups, hoping to enhance and utilize my skills in design, human behavior, and technology. I am self-taught in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and happily learning something new every day.
