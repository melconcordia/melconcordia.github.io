Bike & Build

Summer Cross-Country Cycling Trip Sponsoring Affordable Housing

From 2010-2011 I prepared for and embarked on a 78 day bicycle trip across the Southern U.S. with 33 companions. I fundraised $4,000, trained 700 miles, and volunteered with the local Habitat for Humanity chapter, before meeting the rest of the team.

On May 18, 2011, 34 cyclists met in Jacksonville, FL and embarked on a 2 month journey through some of the country's hottest and most isolated regions, stopping for 16 days to build with local affordable housing agencies.
